Q.1. List the different types of mouse. Explain them.

Ans. The different types of mouse are as follows:

Mechanical Mouse — This type of mouse has a metal or rubber ball on its lower side that can roll in all the directions.

The screen pointer moves according to the movement of the ball which is detected by the mechanical sensors within the mouse.

Opto-Mechanical Mouse — This type of mouse uses a mechanism similar to the mechanical mouse.

The only difference is that it uses optical sensors to detect the movement of the ball.

Optical Mouse — An optical mouse does not have any mechanical moving parts. The laser technology is used to detect the movement of the mouse.
It is more efficient and expensive than the Mechanical and Opto-Mechanical mouse.


Q.2. Differentiate between Impact and Non Impact printers.

Impact Printer

Ans. It forms characters by striking a print hammer against an inked ribbon.

It uses either ribbon or carbon paper. It makes noise while printing.

Non Impact Printers

It forms characters without making direct contact with the paper. It uses ink cartridges. It prints smoothly on the paper.
